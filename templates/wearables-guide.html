🔗 How to Add Your Device Data

1 Open your device's app (Apple Health, Fitbit, Garmin Connect, etc.)
2 Find your daily summary - look for metrics like steps, sleep hours, heart rate
3 Copy the key metrics - our AI reads natural language, so just copy what you see
4 Paste into daily log - add it to the "Wearable Device Data" field
5 Get personalized insights - our AI will analyze patterns and provide recommendations

🤖 What Our AI Does With Your Data

  • Sleep Quality: Correlates sleep with weight loss progress
  • Activity Levels: Optimizes workout intensity based on recovery
  • Heart Rate Variability: Identifies stress and overtraining
  • Step Patterns: Suggests movement throughout the day
  • Hormone Tracking: Adjusts recommendations based on cycle phases
🔒 Privacy Note: Your wearable data is encrypted and only used to provide personalized insights. We never share this information with third parties.
